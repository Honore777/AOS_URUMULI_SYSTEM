Record Cash Transaction

Use this when cash physically enters or leaves the drawer.

What this page is for

This dashboard belongs to the cashier role. It is used to post real cash movements and to collect cash receipts that negotiators recorded for customers.

Requests are first approved by the boss. The cashier then disburses the approved request, which is when the system posts the real cash movement and updates the selected cash account.

That keeps the cash position accurate and synchronized for the boss, accountant, and store teams.

Reminder
If the transaction is not cash movement, do not post it here. Use the negotiator or receipt pages for customer sales flow.

Supplier Advance Refund (Cash In)

Use this when a supplier returns cash for an advance that was given earlier.

How it works

The system records a cash-in transaction to the selected cash account, then creates a negative supplier-advance ledger row for audit.

To keep the supplier wallet correct, the refund is applied FIFO against the supplier’s remaining advances.
